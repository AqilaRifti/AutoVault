# AutoVault - Programmable Savings for the MNEE Economy

## The Problem

Traditional savings accounts are passive and inflexible. Users struggle to:
- Organize savings across multiple goals
- Automate investment strategies like DCA
- Get personalized financial guidance
- Track progress toward financial milestones

## Our Solution: AutoVault

AutoVault is a **programmable savings platform** built on MNEE stablecoin that transforms how people save and invest.
